THE UNITED STATES SENATE

The year is 1943. Dark times. The world is right in the middle of the greatest war it has seen, and there seems to be no end. In this committee, you shall fight the war, and fight to win. The catch: you’re on the losing side of history. You are on the side of history whose actions haunt us to this day. The Axis Cabinet starts on 1st July 1943, just a fews days before Operation Husky was initiated to invade Sicily, Italy. Suffice to say, all is not well. A body, seemingly belonging to that of a British Royal Marine Pilot has been recovered off the Spanish coast; it contained documents enough to convince Hitler that the invasion was toward Greece or Sardinia-Piedmont. But within the Italian military, some believe that the documents were a hoax and the invasion is to take place elsewhere within Italy And they’d like to be prepared for it.
