DISEC

““ If the history of the last fifty years teaches us anything,it is that peace does not follow disarmament- disarmament follows peace.”.”

-Bernard Baruch.

The first committee of the General Assembly, DISEC, presides over topics relating to international security and disarmament. This year's agenda is one of great interest and urgency. The goal of the committee is to dive deeper into the militarization of viruses and the like, as well as address the situation around the 2019-21 pandemic caused by the Covid 19 virus originating in China.

AGENDA

The prevention of biological/chemical warfare, with special reference to Covid 19 and allegations against China.
